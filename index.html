Privacy Policy for MindLockrX

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MindLockrX collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal data when you use the MindLockrX mobile application and related VPN access services.

This policy is written for the MindLockrX mobile application as it currently operates. The mobile app provides authenticated WireGuard VPN access with either full-tunnel or split-tunnel routing. The mobile app does not perform posture checks, does not perform application blocking, and does not perform SSL/TLS interception or MITM decryption.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the MindLockrX mobile app for iOS and Android, including the authentication, control-plane, and VPN connection services required to deliver the app.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Account and Authentication Data

  • Username
  • Email address associated with your account, where available
  • Password entered at sign-in
  • Multi-factor authentication code and related authentication state, where MFA is enabled
  • Authentication tokens generated after successful login

3.2 VPN Provisioning and Session Data

  • WireGuard public key generated for the client session
  • Session ID used to track the active VPN connection
  • Assigned internal VPN IP address
  • Selected VPN gateway identifier and WireGuard endpoint
  • Routing mode and routing parameters required for full-tunnel or split-tunnel operation
  • DNS settings required for the VPN session
  • Connection start time, disconnect time, and disconnect reason
  • Basic VPN connection status and tunnel health information

3.3 Device and Application Data

  • Device model
  • Mobile operating system and OS version
  • Platform type (for example, iOS or Android)
  • Application version
  • A device identity string derived from the device model, platform, and OS version

3.4 Network and Security Log Data

  • Request timestamps
  • Authentication and connection event logs
  • Source IP address seen by the authentication or control services
  • Error logs and operational diagnostics required to operate and secure the VPN service

3.5 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Access token
  • Basic user profile data returned after login
  • Configured controller and authentication server addresses
  • Saved VPN session state needed to restore the in-app connection screen

This local data is stored using secure local storage provided by the mobile operating system where supported.

4. Data We Do Not Collect Through the Mobile App

The current MindLockrX mobile app does not collect or transmit the following categories of data as part of its mobile VPN flow:

  • Posture-check reports
  • Installed application inventory
  • Application blocking or application usage telemetry
  • Biometric posture or behavioral profiling data
  • Camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, call logs, or precise location data
  • Advertising ID
  • SSL/TLS MITM interception, content decryption, or user-installed inspection certificates

5. How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and enforce account security
  • To provide WireGuard VPN connectivity
  • To assign VPN session identifiers and internal addresses
  • To route traffic according to full-tunnel or split-tunnel VPN configuration
  • To configure DNS and gateway information needed for the VPN connection
  • To detect service abuse, misuse, failures, and security incidents
  • To troubleshoot operational issues and improve reliability
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to valid lawful requests

6. How VPN Traffic Is Handled

MindLockrX uses WireGuard to create a secure tunnel between your mobile device and the VPN gateway selected for your session.

  • In full-tunnel mode, network traffic from the device is routed through the VPN according to the configuration provided for your session.
  • In split-tunnel mode, only configured destinations or routes are sent through the VPN.

The mobile app uses this routing information only to establish and maintain the VPN session. The mobile app does not inspect or decrypt SSL/TLS traffic through MITM.

8. How We Share Data

We may share relevant data only as necessary with:

  • Authentication infrastructure used to verify your credentials and MFA
  • Control-plane services that provision and manage VPN sessions
  • VPN gateway systems that need the session configuration to establish your WireGuard tunnel
  • Hosting, networking, security, and infrastructure service providers acting on our behalf
  • Professional advisers, auditors, or legal authorities where required by law
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or transfer of all or part of the business

We do not sell your personal data and we do not use the mobile app for advertising profiling.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including service delivery, account security, troubleshooting, and legal compliance.

  • Local app data generally remains on your device until logout, reset, or app removal.
  • Authentication and VPN session records may be retained as needed for security, auditing, and support.
  • Operational and security logs may be retained for a limited period consistent with business and legal needs.
  • Backups may retain residual copies for a limited time before routine deletion.
